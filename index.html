Founded in 2020 during the global COVID-19 pandemic, the Lina Nkosi Foundation emerged as a beacon of hope in challenging times. While many faced uncertainty and despair we KEPT OUR FAITH, our unwavering commitment was to stand ready to assist wherever help was needed. We remain dedicated to uplifting communities and fostering resilience through every obstacle.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mission of the Lina Nkosi Foundation organisation?

The mission of the Lina Nkosi Foundation is to support vulnerable communities by providing resources, education, and services that foster social and economic empowerment.

What types of initiatives does the organisation support?

The organisation focuses on initiatives related to education, healthcare, poverty alleviation, community development, and women's empowerment.

How can I get involved with the organisation?

You can get involved by volunteering, donating, or partnering with the organisation. Visit our 'Contact Us' section to leave your details, and our trusted agent will be in touch. You can also connect with us through our social media platforms.

Where does the Lina Nkosi Foundation organisation operate?

We primarily operate in underserved communities in South Africa, but our programs and initiatives have the potential to expand to other regions.

How are donations used?

Donations are used to fund our various programs, including educational scholarships, healthcare services, food drives, and housing projects. We ensure that all funds are allocated responsibly.

How do I stay updated on the foundation's work?

You can stay updated by clicking the subcribe button or following us on social media. We regularly post updates about ongoing projects and events.

Can companies or organisations partner with the Lina Nkosi Foundation?

Yes, we welcome partnerships with companies, organisation, and other foundations that align with our mission and values. Reach out to us to explore partnership opportunities.
